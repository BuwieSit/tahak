Tahak: Tahanan sa Kalikasan

Rooted in Dolores, Quezon, TahaK is an eco wellness brand unlike any other—crafted from the wisdom of Filipino ancestors, the abundance of the land, and the rhythm of nature.

What is TahaK?

TahaK (Tahanan sa Kalikasan) is the first eco wellness brand offering curated packages in Dolores, Quezon, a province in the Philippines, born from a vision to create a sanctuary where nature becomes a home for travelers seeking healing and transformation.

Why choose TahaK?

At TahaK, we offer a holistic wellness retreat through tour packages located in Dolores, Quezon, designed to offer immersive and transformative experiences that foster self-discovery, healing, and connection with self, nature and community.

About Dolores, Quezon

Dolores is known as a faith and wellness destination

— a mystical gateway town where Catholic devotion, indigenous mysticism, eco-tourism, and mountain spirituality intertwine.
