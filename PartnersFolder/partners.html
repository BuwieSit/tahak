Partners

Peace in Nature Farm Resort To successfully launch and grow the enterprise, we also need to involve resort and ecotourism site owners like Peace in Nature Resort, who provide the physical space for our wellness experiences. They provide private swimming pool, farm, and local fruits which they have grown naturally.

Banahaw Circle Nature Retreat For space and logistics, we established a partnership with a local accommodation, Banahaw Circle Nature Retreat, in Dolores, Quezon, which serves as our pilot site and sanctuary for wellness activities—offering guests a peaceful, nature-integrated environment

Non-government Organizations (NGOs)

Luntiang Alyansa para sa Bundok Banahaw (LABB) An NGO dedicated to protecting and restoring the biodiversity of Mount Banahaw. Also, they are committed to ecological preservation and spiritual stewardship of Mt. Banahaw.

KALIPI (Kalipunan ng Liping Pilipina) A local women’s organization committed to empowerment, livelihood, and sustainable development.
